FRANKFURT INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Grade 10–12 Athlete Academy

Nomination 2026–27

The Athlete Academy is a multi-year development programme for students who are ready to take ownership of their athletic growth. Selected students train with purpose, track their progress over time, and develop the physical and psychological foundations of a complete athlete. Nominations are reviewed by the PE department. Places are limited.

THE PROGRAMME

What your athlete will have access to

Training Age

A personalised composite score across Psychology, Physiology, and Health & Skills. Updated every term. Grows across years in the programme.

Personal Training Portal

Athletes plan their own training weeks, choose from coach templates, log sessions, and track intensity. Everything visible to the coach.

Coach Check-ins & Booking

Book in-person slots before or after school. Personal calendar with group testing days and programme reviews. Downloads straight to Google Calendar.

Foundation Strength Patterns

Six fundamental movement patterns tracked progressively across five levels. Technique assessed, strength logged every session.

Goals, Vision & Grit

Athletes set a long-term vision and active goals across fitness, strength and sport. Weekly reflections feed a transparent Grit score — they can see exactly what drives it.

Long-term Development Tracking

Training Age tracked from programme entry through G12. Every term adds a data point. Students see where they've come from and where they're heading.
