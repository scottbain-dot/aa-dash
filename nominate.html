FRANKFURT INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Grade 10–12 Athlete Academy

Nomination 2026–27

The Athlete Academy is a multi-year development programme for students who are ready to take ownership of their athletic growth. Selected students train with purpose, track their progress over time, and develop the physical and psychological foundations of a complete athlete. Nominations are reviewed by the PE department. Places are limited.

THE PROGRAMME

What your athlete will have access to

Training Age

A personalised metric that tracks athletic development across Psychology, Physiology, and Health & Skills. Updated every term. Grows over years.

Personal Training Portal

Athletes plan their own training weeks, log sessions, and track progress over time. Teacher-visible — coach feedback built in.

PE Time to Train

Selected PE units give athletes dedicated training time during the school day — structured development built into the timetable, not just an after-school commitment.

Strength & Movement Foundations

Progressive technique and strength development across six fundamental movement patterns. Assessed termly, tracked across years.
