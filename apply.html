The Athlete Academy is for high-potential student-athletes who are ready to take ownership of their athletic development.

Students plan and carry out their own training blocks, guided by a purpose-built system that tracks progress across physical and psychological measures. Regular check-ins, termly testing, and structured challenges keep development on track.

HOW IT WORKS

What to expect

01

Apply

Submit your application below. Your coach or teacher will also need to nominate you separately.

02

Selection

Selected students are contacted before the end of term. Places are offered based on attitude, potential, and commitment — not just ability.

03

Start of Programme

You'll be assessed, set your goals, and start building your Training Age from day one.

04

Keep Going

Check-ins, testing days, and your own training blocks. Your progress is tracked and visible every step of the way.

THE PROGRAMME

What you'll have access to

Training Age

A score that's yours. It tracks how you're developing as an athlete across three areas — and it grows every term you're in the programme.

Personal Training Portal

Plan your own training weeks, log your sessions, and see your progress over time. Your coach can see everything — so can you.

PE Time to Train

Selected PE units give you dedicated time during the school day to train your way.

Strength & Movement

Six fundamental movement patterns, tracked from Level 1 to Level 5. Every session counts.

Grit Challenge

Every term you take on an 8-session challenge you design yourself. Push your limits, track your progress, and prove what you're made of.

Earn Your Kit

Progress through Training Age levels and unlock Warriors × Athlete Academy kit. What you earn depends on how far you go.
